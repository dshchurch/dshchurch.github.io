Duffus Spynie and Hopeman Church

Church Magazine

The congregation’s magazine enables a sharing of news and views, with contributions welcome from everyone. These may relate to the communities we live in and serve, the church locally, nationally and globally, discussion of topical issues, recipes, jokes, stories, poems, history, and almost anything.

To contribute to the church magazine, you can contact the minister at jadams@churchofScotland.org.uk
